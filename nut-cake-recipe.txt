Ingredients:
- 250 grams sugar
- 250 grams almond meal
- 250 grams flour
- 250 ml white wine
- Pinch of salt
- Nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves to taste

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 8" square pan.

2. In a large bowl, combine the dry ingredients. Stir in white wine, and mix batter until uniform. Pour batter into prepared pan.

3. Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool overnight. Optionally add glazure. Serve with coffee or wine.
